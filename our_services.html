Our Services

Change the size

Ring size is just as important as the ring itself. A suitable and comfortable ring will enhance the relationship with an elegant ring. Royalshine will not give up until you are satisfied in every way,therefore we offer free resizing for engraved or unengraved rings or one time free of charge within the first 60 days of receipt your product.

Free Engraving

Give a private or personal message with a free engraving request and give the present life with a sentimental value. Make her feel special and exclusive - just like her ring.

Royalshine will engrave your ring for free when you choose this option during purchase. You can choose from four different fonts and include special symbols like hearts or infinity. The maximum number of symbols you can enter is 25 - including spaces, symbols, and letters.

Lifetime warranty

Royalshine stands out in the industry with an attractive lifetime warranty. Wedding or engagement rings are purchases for a lifetime, so we want to make sure that you receive top quality products with a guarantee. A lifetime warranty applies to all Royalshine jewelry, because we believe in our products. In the event of any manufacturer defect, Royalshine will inspect the product and will gladly repair it for you. Please refer to our return policy for more details.

Free shipping insurance

Jewelry is precious, valuable and requires special care during delivery. Royalshine offers additional insurance and coverage for all orders that are lost or damaged in transit.

We recommend that you open your package and check it for damage. If you discover any damage, please file a claim for damages and refuse to sign the delivery note.

Online refund

If you are not satisfied with your order, you are given the opportunity to return the item to Royalshine or for a refund, resizing, engraving or repair request. Submit return orders just a few simple clicks. Print it out and send it back. Once the return form is submitted, you will receive instructions to your email address on how to proceed with the return. Please read the return policy before sending it back to us.
